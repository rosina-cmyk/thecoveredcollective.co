What We Do

Four functions. One purpose.

TCC is not a broker, not a charity, not a consultancy. We convene, structure, and hold the promise — operating across four functions that together move communities from outside the system to inside it.

01 — Voice

We translate risk into language the market can work with.

TCC goes to communities currently outside the insurance system and listens. Working with our GIS partner, we map exposure and turn lived risk into data that underwriters can price, structure and act on.

02 — Assembly

We bring the right people to the same table.

TCC convenes the market participants who can respond — brokers, (re)insurers, parametric specialists, insurtechs. Not a conference. A structured conversation with a specific outcome.

03 — Structure

We build the conditions for solutions to exist.

TCC facilitates the creation of the fund, pool, or product. We do not own it. We build the conditions in which it can exist — and step back. The solution belongs to the community and the market.

04 — The Pledge

We hold the market to account.

Capital providers who participate in a TCC-facilitated fund make a named, public, time-bound commitment that the fund will remain open for a minimum term. TCC holds and publishes the record.

"Solving insurtech adoption does not close the protection gap. The barriers are economic, structural, cultural, and political — not only technical. TCC exists to address what remains after the adoption problem is solved."

The Pledge

Named. Public. Time-bound. On the record.

The Pledge is built on a simple principle: accountability has to outlast the announcement. Capital providers who participate in a TCC-facilitated fund make a public, recorded commitment that the fund will remain open for a minimum term. TCC holds that record. We publish it.
